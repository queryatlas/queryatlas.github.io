About Us

At Queryatlas, we're building AI-powered SaaS tools that help sales engineers and product teams work smarter, faster, and more in sync. In B2B tech, sales and product alignment is everything—but too often, it's slowed down by manual work, scattered tools, and missed context. We’re here to fix that. Our platform uses AI to streamline technical sales workflows, auto-generate product content, and surface insights that help teams close deals and ship features with confidence. No more copy-pasting from docs, chasing down updates, or reinventing the wheel before every demo or RFP. What We Believe: 🧠 AI should do the grunt work, so your team can focus on strategy, not spreadsheets. 🔄 Sales and product should move together, not in silos. ⚙️ SaaS should be simple, powerful, and built for how teams actually work. We’re a fast-moving team of engineers, product leaders, and AI enthusiasts building with real users in mind. If your sales and product teams are ready for a better way to collaborate, we’d love to show you what’s possible. Reach out to us at contact@queryatlas.com
